Equipment

We provide a comprehensive selection of fitness equipment, including dumbbells ranging from 1-60kg, a wide variety of barbells, and a complete set of professional equipment. This diverse range is designed to help you enhance your strength, balance, and mobility, facilitating the achievement of a healthy and your desired body shape.

Cardio

Get ready to conquer over 50 cardio machines that we've got waiting for you! From treadmills to steppers, bikes to rowing machines, and stair climbers, we've got the full squad to make your heart race and say goodbye to that stubborn fat. It's not just a workout; it's a party for your heart and a farewell bash for those extra calories!

Yoga
