Our Classes

We provide a diverse array of classes tailored to enhance your well-being, with exhilarating choices such as Cardio, Yoga, Crossfit, and small group training available on various weekdays to accommodate your schedule. Each session lasts for 45 minutes, ensuring an effective and efficient workout experience.

Class Schedule

Cadio

Monday | 7pm

Our Cardio class boosts heart health and energy through dynamic aerobics and rhythmic exercises. Suitable for all levels, it's a calorie-burning, endurance-building workout designed to elevate your fitness journey in a motivating atmosphere.

Group Training

Wednesday | 7pm

Our group Training focuses on strength and muscle building in a collaborative environment. Through structured workouts using weights, participants work on various muscle groups, improving overall strength, tone, and endurance. Ideal for all fitness levels, it's a great way to enhance your physical conditioning while being part of a supportive community.

Yoga

Tuesday and Thursday | 7pm

Our yoga Class offers a serene environment to enhance flexibility, balance, and inner peace. Through various poses and breathing techniques, participants of all skill levels can improve their physical and mental well-being, reduce stress, and increase mindfulness. Join us to embark on a journey toward a more harmonious self.

Equipment

Monday and Friday | 6pm

We offer a wide range of fitness equipment, from 1-60kg dumbbells to various barbells and professional-grade gear. Our diverse selection supports your strength, balance, and mobility goals, helping you achieve your desired body shape and overall health.
